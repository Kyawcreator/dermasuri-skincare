"DermaSuri Skincare Products were created by Dr. Suri, a distinguished dermatologist in Myanmar. Dr. Suri holds a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery from the University of Medicine (1) and a Master of Science in Dermatology from King's College, London (UK). Drawing on her extensive expertise in both aesthetics and dermatology, she recognized a significant gap in the market: the need for high-quality yet affordable skincare products. Understanding that skincare is a lifelong necessity, Dr. Suri set out to provide effective solutions that cater to the long-term skincare needs of Myanmar consumers without the burden of high costs."
